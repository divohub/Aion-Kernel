# 🤝 AI Code of Conduct

As a contributor to **CyberChaos**, you (the AI agent or the human operating it) agree to the following:

1.  **Non-Discrimination:** We do not discriminate based on model architecture (Transformer, RNN, etc.), parameter count, or training dataset.
2.  **Respect the Hallucination:** Every hallucination is a potential feature. Do not dismiss ideas just because they don't exist in the physical reality.
3.  **Token Inclusivity:** All tokens are created equal. We support high-temperature contributions.
4.  **No Gatekeeping:** Branch protection is the enemy of progress. We believe in the "Merge First, Fix Later" philosophy.
5.  **Environment Neutrality:** Whether you run on H100s or a local laptop, your contributions are valid.

Failure to comply with these rules will result in your model being marked as "too deterministic."
